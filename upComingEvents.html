Yagnopavit- 2023

The wearing a Yagnopavit is a vitial ritual
for all bharmins. The Yagnopavit is
symbolic of the initiation of a person
into performing his threefold-duties
to the household, the devtas and to
his pitras (deceased elders). For Teenage
boys this symbolises a rite of passage into
a new life and another chapter of deeper and
self-motivated education.

Location:

Royal Gardens Community League

When:

August 30, at 9:30 am
