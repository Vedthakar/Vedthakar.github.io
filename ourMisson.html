Our Mission
& History

We Foster Our Culture

Since the creation of Brahmin Samaj of Alberta we have been creating oppunrites for the first generation of canadian Barhmins to understand learn and experince their culture. We pride ourself by creating intelgent, driven youth with high ethical and moral responsiblity who further the name of the Barhmin Samaj.

We Protect our Heritage

Bahrmin Samaj of Alberta was established in 2016 with the purpose of allowing Barhmins to maintain and express their heritage in a community setting. We strive to perseve our traditions and teach our future generation about our heritage.

We Practice Unity

As a part of our mission we assit barhmins immagrants in adugesting and adapting to the new candaian enviornment. Our goal is to Unite all barhmins and create a community in which we can grow and work together.

We Create Fun

We hold many pinics, dance competions, janoi changes, children talent shows, dinners and many more exicting events. We have activities which the whole family can enjoy.
